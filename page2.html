Welcome To The 2022-2023 Track Season!

In Track And Field, You will be competing in events that take place on the running track. These events are a wide range of sprinting and distance. There are also events that take place in the infield. These events focus more on upper body strength Such as throwing. The following text below this introduction answers any questions you might have about this season.

Luckily, there are no tryouts in order to be part of the track team! However this does not mean that everyone who is part of the team can go to meets. Only some of the best runners will go.

Varsity letters will be awarded to athletes who have shown great dedication and achievement. These letters can be awarded in two ways. The first way is to go to a championship meet such as countys are shores. The second way they can be earned is to get a varsity time for an event. These times will be listed on the next page.

The only important rules to follow competing in this sport are the following: to treat every team member with respect, go to every meet and practice and if you don't go email the coach, Don't beat yourself up if you think you did bad in a meet,
